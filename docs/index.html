"My Teacher" is an enterprise-grade pedagogical tool designed to bridge the gap in Kannada-medium education. It transforms passive reading into an active, trilingual mastery experience for students aged 10-16.

Learn the Pedagogy

The Pedagogical Aspect

Beyond Verbatim Readout.

Most educational tools focus on translation. My Teacher focuses on Contextual Mastery.

For students in the 10-16 bracket, hearing a word is not enough. They need to understand the soul of the paragraph. Our engine combines verbatim reading with a teacher’s explanation, providing a personal tutor experience in every lesson.

Core Principles

  • ▹ Optical Clarity: Large-format Kannada script (24px) to prevent eye strain.
  • ▹ The 1-Second Gap: Intentional silence between reading and explaining to allow cognitive processing.
  • ▹ Trilingual Bridge: Simultaneously learning in Kannada, Hindi, and English.
